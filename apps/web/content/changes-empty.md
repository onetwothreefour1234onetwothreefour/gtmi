Policy change detection ships in Phase&nbsp;5. Once live, every Tier&nbsp;1
government source we track will be re-scraped weekly. Detected changes
appear here within 24 hours, classified by severity (minor / material /
breaking), with diffs and Wayback-archived snapshots of the page state
before and after the change.

Subscribers receive email alerts via Resend on material and breaking
changes. The summary text is generated by Claude and reviewed by a human
before publication.

Phase&nbsp;5 also lights up the per-programme policy timeline on each
program detail page — the timeline component is already in place, waiting
on the data.
